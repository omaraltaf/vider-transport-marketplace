# Vider Transport Marketplace - Project Overview

## Executive Summary

**Vider** (Norwegian for "Further" / "Onward") is a production-ready, peer-to-peer marketplace platform specifically designed for the Norwegian B2B transport and logistics market. The platform enables transport companies to efficiently share resources, rent vehicles and drivers to one another, and optimize their fleet utilization while building a trusted network of industry partners.

## üöõ What is Vider?

Vider is a comprehensive digital marketplace that connects transport companies across Norway, enabling them to:

- **Rent vehicles** to other transport companies during peak demand or equipment shortages
- **Share driver services** when additional manpower is needed
- **Optimize fleet utilization** by monetizing idle assets
- **Build trusted business relationships** through a transparent rating and review system
- **Streamline operations** with automated booking, contract management, and invoicing
