# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: What is the Platform Admin Dashboard?
**A:** The Platform Admin Dashboard is a comprehensive administrative interface for managing the Vider Transport Marketplace platform. It provides tools for user management, company oversight, financial operations, analytics, content moderation, and security monitoring.

### Q: Who can access the Platform Admin Dashboard?
**A:** Access is restricted to authorized platform administrators with proper credentials and role assignments. Different admin roles have different levels of access to various features.

### Q: How do I get admin access?
**A:** Admin access is granted by senior platform administrators. Contact your supervisor or the IT department to request access with proper justification.

## Login and Security

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**A:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

### Q: Why is Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) required?
**A:** MFA provides an additional layer of security for admin accounts, which have access to sensitive platform data and operations. It's required for all admin accounts to protect against unauthorized access.

### Q: My MFA device is lost/broken. How do I regain access?
**A:** Use your backup codes if available, or contact the IT support team for MFA reset. You'll need to verify your identity through alternative means.

### Q: How long do admin sessions last?
**A:** Admin sessions typically last 24 hours of inactivity. You'll be automatically logged out for security reasons and will need to log in again.

## User Management

### Q: How do I create a new admin user?
**A:** Go to User Management → Admin Users → Create New Admin. Fill in the required information, assign appropriate roles, and send the invitation. The new admin will receive setup instructions via email.

### Q: What's the difference between Platform Admin, Company Admin, and Company User roles?
**A:** 
- **Platform Admin**: Full access to all platform features and data
- **Company Admin**: Limited to managing their own company's data and users
- **Company User**: Basic access to their own profile and company information

### Q: Can I change a user's role after creation?
**A:** Yes, Platform Admins can modify user roles. Go to the user's profile and update their role. This action is logged in the audit trail.

### Q: How do I suspend a user account?
**A:** Navigate to the user's profile, click "Suspend Account," provide a reason, and confirm. The user will be immediately logged out and unable to access the platform.

## Company Management

### Q: How long does company verification take?
**A:** Company verification typically takes 1-3 business days, depending on the completeness of submitted documents and current queue volume.

### Q: What documents are required for company verification?
**A:** Required documents include:
- Business registration certificate
- VAT registration (if applicable)
- Proof of business address
- Authorized signatory identification
- Insurance documentation (for transport companies)

### Q: Can I reject a company application?
**A:** Yes, you can reject applications that don't meet platform requirements. Always provide clear reasons for rejection and guidance on how to reapply successfully.

### Q: How do I handle company compliance issues?
**A:** Document the compliance issue, contact the company for clarification, set a resolution timeline, and follow up. Serious violations may require temporary suspension pending resolution.

## Financial Management

### Q: How are commission rates determined?
**A:** Commission rates are set based on platform policy, market conditions, and business agreements. Only senior platform admins can modify commission rates.

### Q: How do I process a refund?
**A:** Navigate to the transaction in question, review the refund request details, verify the reason, calculate the appropriate refund amount, and process through the financial management interface.

### Q: What's the typical dispute resolution timeframe?
**A:** Most disputes are resolved within 5-7 business days. Complex cases may take longer and might require additional investigation or external consultation.

### Q: Can I reverse a processed refund?
**A:** Refund reversals are complex and typically require senior admin approval and coordination with the finance team. Contact support for assistance.

## Analytics and Reporting

### Q: How often is analytics data updated?
**A:** Most analytics data is updated in real-time or near real-time. Some complex reports may have a delay of up to 15 minutes.

### Q: Can I schedule automated reports?
**A:** Yes, you can schedule daily, weekly, or monthly reports to be automatically generated and emailed to specified recipients.

### Q: What's the maximum date range for analytics queries?
**A:** You can query up to 2 years of historical data. For older data or special analysis, contact the data team.

### Q: How do I export large datasets?
**A:** For large exports, use the bulk export feature with appropriate filters. Very large datasets may need to be processed in chunks or scheduled during off-peak hours.

## Content Moderation

### Q: How do I handle inappropriate content reports?
**A:** Review the reported content, assess against platform guidelines, take appropriate action (approve, remove, or escalate), and notify the reporting user of the outcome.

### Q: What constitutes a safety violation?
**A:** Safety violations include unsafe vehicles, unlicensed drivers, fraudulent listings, harassment, or any content that could endanger users or violate regulations.

### Q: Can users appeal moderation decisions?
**A:** Yes, users can appeal through the support system. Appeals are reviewed by senior moderators or platform admins.

### Q: How do I add items to the blacklist?
**A:** Navigate to Content Moderation → Blacklist → Add Item. Specify the type (user, email, phone, etc.), provide the value, and include a reason for blacklisting.

## Security and Monitoring

### Q: What should I do if I see a security alert?
**A:** Immediately review the alert details, assess the threat level, take appropriate containment actions if needed, investigate the cause, and document your response.

### Q: How long are audit logs retained?
**A:** Audit logs are retained for 7 years to meet compliance requirements. Older logs are archived but remain accessible for legal or regulatory purposes.

### Q: What triggers a security alert?
**A:** Security alerts are triggered by various events including multiple failed logins, unusual access patterns, privilege escalation attempts, bulk data access, and system anomalies.

### Q: How do I investigate a security incident?
**A:** Use the Security Dashboard to review related events, check audit logs for the timeframe, analyze user activity patterns, and document your findings. Escalate serious incidents to the security team.

## System Administration

### Q: How do I check system health?
**A:** The System Health dashboard provides real-time status of all platform components including databases, servers, external services, and performance metrics.

### Q: Can I enable maintenance mode?
**A:** Only senior platform admins can enable maintenance mode. This should be coordinated with the technical team and users should be notified in advance.

### Q: How do I manage feature toggles?
**A:** Feature toggles can be managed through System Administration → Feature Toggles. Changes should be made carefully and tested in staging first.

### Q: What backup procedures are in place?
**A:** Automated backups run daily with retention policies. Manual backups can be initiated for major changes. Recovery procedures are documented in the disaster recovery plan.

## Technical Issues

### Q: The dashboard is loading slowly. What should I do?
**A:** Check your internet connection, clear browser cache, try a different browser, or check the system status page. If issues persist, contact technical support.

### Q: I'm getting permission errors. What's wrong?
**A:** Verify your role and permissions, ensure you're accessing the correct resources, check if your session has expired, or contact an admin to verify your access rights.

### Q: Can I use the dashboard on mobile devices?
**A:** The dashboard is optimized for desktop use but has basic mobile compatibility. For full functionality, use a desktop or laptop computer.

### Q: Which browsers are supported?
**A:** Supported browsers include Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, and Edge 90+. Always use the latest version for best performance and security.

## Training and Support

### Q: Is training available for new admins?
**A:** Yes, comprehensive training is provided for new platform admins including live sessions, documentation, and hands-on practice in a training environment.

### Q: How do I stay updated on new features?
**A:** New features are announced through email notifications, in-app announcements, and the changelog. Subscribe to admin updates to stay informed.

### Q: Where can I find additional documentation?
**A:** Complete documentation is available in the admin dashboard help section, the knowledge base, and the developer documentation portal.

### Q: How do I provide feedback or request features?
**A:** Use the feedback form in the dashboard, email suggestions to product@vider.no, or discuss ideas in the admin user group meetings.
